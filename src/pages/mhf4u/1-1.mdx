## Polynomial and Power Functions

A **polynomial function** is a function in the form of:

$ f(x)= a_nx^n+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}+a_{n-2}x^{n-2}+a_{n-3}x^{n-3}+a_1x+a_0 $

where $$n$$ is a whole positive number and is the **degree**, $$x$$ is a variable, $$a_{n}, a_{n-1}â€¦$$ are real number **coefficients**, $$a_{0}$$ is a **constant term**.

A **power function** is a function in the form of f(x)=ax^n.  A power function is the simplest form of a polynomial function.
